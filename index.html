Elon Musk

Biography

Elon Musk is a business magnate, industrial designer, and engineer. He is the CEO and founder of SpaceX and the co-founder, CEO, and product architect of Tesla, Inc. He is also the co-founder and CEO of Neuralink and The Boring Company.

Before his success with SpaceX and Tesla, Musk co-founded Zip2, a web software company that was later sold to Compaq for $307 million in 1999. He also co-founded PayPal, which was later sold to eBay for $1.5 billion in 2002.

In addition to his business ventures, Musk has been vocal about his vision for the future, which includes making humanity a "multi-planetary species" and reducing the use of fossil fuels through sustainable energy sources.
